Projects

Submontium

A narrative survival RPG about an expedition from Mariánské Lázně to Antarctica. Single‑player. PC. Unreal Engine 5.6.

Genre: Survival RPG / Narrative Engine: Unreal Engine 5.6.1 Platform: PC (Steam) Status: In development

Vision

  • Weatherbound design. Climate and terrain are not background—they are the main system. Forecast, plan, survive.
  • Science over violence. Your toolkit is barometers, drills and labs—not guns.
  • 1930s Art Deco. A grounded, handcrafted aesthetic with modern fidelity.
  • Expedition dramaturgy. From Mariánské Lázně to Patagonia.

About

Submontium Games is a small studio focused on handcrafted visuals and original systems. We build with Unreal Engine, Blender, and Mari. The studio is based in the Czech Republic and led by Zdeněk Levý.

We create narrative survival games where memory, weather and science shape the player’s journey. Our current project, Submontium, begins at the Mariánské Lázně colonnade and culminates on the Antarctic plateau.
